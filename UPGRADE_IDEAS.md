This document contains ideas for future upgrades and weapons to expand the game.

## üéØ New Weapon Ideas

### 1. **Candy Cane Spear**
- **Type**: Melee weapon
- **Description**: A spinning candy cane that orbits the player
- **Mechanics**: 
  - Rotates around player at medium distance
  - Deals damage on contact
  - Can be upgraded for multiple spears
  - Synergizes with attack speed upgrades

### 2. **Gingerbread Turret**
- **Type**: Stationary defense
- **Description**: Deploys gingerbread men that shoot at nearby enemies
- **Mechanics**:
  - Places turrets that last 30 seconds
  - Auto-targets closest enemy
  - Can have multiple active at once
  - Upgrades increase damage and duration

### 3. **Icicle Rain**
- **Type**: Area attack
- **Description**: Summons icicles that fall from the sky
- **Mechanics**:
  - Targets random enemies in range
  - High damage, moderate cooldown
  - Upgrades increase icicle count
  - Pierces through enemies

### 4. **Snowflake Boomerang**
- **Type**: Projectile
- **Description**: Throws snowflakes that return to player
- **Mechanics**:
  - Flies out and returns
  - Damages enemies on both paths
  - Can hit same enemy twice
  - Upgrades add more boomerangs

### 5. **Hot Cocoa Bomb**
- **Type**: Explosive
- **Description**: Throws explosive cocoa mugs
- **Mechanics**:
  - Explodes on impact or after distance
  - Area damage with knockback
  - Leaves burning puddle
  - Upgrades increase radius and burn duration

## ‚≠ê New Upgrade Ideas

### Offensive Upgrades

#### **Frostbite**
- Snowballs apply a stacking slow effect
- Each hit slows enemy by 5% (max 50%)
- Synergizes with multishot

#### **Critical Strike**
- +3% chance to deal 3x damage
- Upgrades increase crit chance and multiplier
- Works with all weapons

#### **Chain Lightning**
- Snowballs have 25% chance to chain to nearby enemies
- Chains up to 3 times
- Each chain deals 50% damage

#### **Explosive Snowballs**
- Snowballs explode on impact
- Small AoE damage
- Upgrades increase radius

#### **Homing Shots**
- Projectiles slightly track enemies
- More effective at long range
- Stacks with multishot

### Defensive Upgrades

#### **Second Wind**
- When health drops below 25%, gain temporary invincibility (3s)
- Cooldown: 60 seconds
- One-time upgrade

#### **Thorns**
- Enemies take damage when hitting you
- Returns 50% of damage taken
- Upgrades increase return damage

#### **Shield Regeneration**
- Gain a shield that regenerates when not taking damage
- Shield absorbs 50 damage
- Regenerates after 5 seconds without damage


#### **Fortify**
- Reduce all damage taken by 15%
- Stacks additively with other damage reduction
- Can be upgraded multiple times

### Utility Upgrades


#### **Lucky Clover**
- Increases rarity of upgrade drops
- Higher chance for Epic/Legendary upgrades
- Affects present drops
